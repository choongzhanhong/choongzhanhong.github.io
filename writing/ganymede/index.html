Ganymede

20 Mar 2026

Tags: #writing #sci-fi

[21E9, F2 Jov 09:A0]: I made a voyage to Ganymede on the orders of Capt. Tsing. Actually, it was a personal request, but the order had to be filed. It would normally be highly unlikely for any personnel to visit Ganymede during the quarantine, and only those with a clearance level above URV-6, or granted permissions by personnel holding such privileges, such as the captain, are allowed to visit.

The instructions point to a coördinate, which was the location of an older voyage that occurred shortly before the moon was closed. The mission dates back to some years before my enlistment to the corps.

[21E9, F7 Jov 12:01]: Clearance has been granted and the necessary paperwork filed.

This story is based on a dream I had. Much of it was transposed without change from what I recall of the dream.
