If you build it, they will come04 Dec 2024

I think I first read this (misattributed) quote from an achievement in Team Fortress 2, or perhaps somewhere else, but it know for a fact it wasn't from the movie, which I haven't seen anyway. Still, it stuck with me, perhaps in part due to its absurdity to me at the time (build what? who will come?).

Anyway, for a good part of my life this saying has sort of become a mantra to me to get me to initiate stuff, especially with friends. I had also been twiddling my thumbs about getting around to writing a blog for the longest time, but never being satisfied with how to do it. As such, I have some writings in Blogspot/Blogger, others in Medium, Tumblr... and now I'm back here on my own website in plain HTML, which isn't too bad. If I ever decide to uproot everything again, at least it's already all formatted for me.

I'll try to hold myself to writing at least something every week.
