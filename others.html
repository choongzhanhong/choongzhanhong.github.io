Academic Plan Template

A spreadsheet mainly intended for NUS students to map their modules. Features dynamic CAP calculation and module highlighting. Also includes a tutorial ranking table. Feel free to save a copy and alter for your own uses.

NUS Blog

A blog run by me and my friends where we review the modules we have taken.

Water for my Family

A map of all the public water coolers in Singapore. I began this project because the weather is hot here, and I want potable water to be more accessible to the public. I welcome any additions to this water map as I have yet to check most places.
