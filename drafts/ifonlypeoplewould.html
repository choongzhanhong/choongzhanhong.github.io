Singapore has a "if only people would" problem05 Dec 2024

Outside of Marina Bay MRT, there once stood a barrier that forced commuters to take a detour to exit the train station to get to work. Naturally, no one actually did that, instead opting to hop over the barrier and forming a desire path on the grass leading up to it.

Some articles cite the barrier's purpose being to block motorcyclists from parking illegally. In any case, the barrier was eventually taken down.
